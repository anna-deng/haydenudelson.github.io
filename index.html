Sypnosis

Our task is to predict the box office revenue of movies based on a series of attributes (such as budget, genre, etc). By making a model to predict if films will be profitable, we will be able to determine which attributes play the greatest role in the ultimate success of a film. It would be important for studios to know what factors can help them compete against others and to be able to accurately predict how much money they expect their upcoming films will make, so they can budget accordingly and maximize the revenue from the box office.
